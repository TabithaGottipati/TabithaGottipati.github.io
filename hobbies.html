My Hobbies

Outside of engineering, I love exploring creative outlets, staying active, and learning new things. Each section below highlights one of my main hobbies — with space for detailed activity cards.

🎨 Art & Design

I enjoy sketching, digital illustration, and experimenting with color, composition, and visual storytelling. Here are a few of the creative activities I explore.

Digital Illustration

I create character art, concept sketches, and stylized portraits using Procreate and Adobe Illustrator.

Sketching & Traditional Art

Pencil and ink sketching helps me practice shading, anatomy, and composition without relying on digital tools.

Graphic Design

I design posters, logos, and layouts, focusing on typography, color harmony, and clean visual hierarchy.

🏋️ Fitness

Strength training, running, and staying active help me stay grounded and energized.

🎵 Music

I love discovering new artists, curating playlists, and going to concerts.

✈️ Travel

Exploring new places, cultures, and foods is one of my favorite ways to learn.
